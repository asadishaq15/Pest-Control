Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pest control services

Are your pest control treatments safe for pets and children?

Yes, we use eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets. Our technicians are trained to apply treatments in a way that minimizes exposure to humans and pets. We'll provide specific instructions on when it's safe to re-enter treated areas.

How often should I schedule pest control services?

The frequency depends on your specific situation and pest pressures. For most homes, quarterly treatments (every 3 months) provide excellent year-round protection. Businesses may require monthly service. After inspection, we'll recommend the optimal schedule for your property.

Do I need to prepare my home before a pest control treatment?

Yes, there are typically a few simple preparations needed. We'll provide you with a detailed preparation checklist prior to your appointment. Common preparations include clearing clutter, moving furniture from walls, and ensuring pets are secured. This helps us provide the most effective treatment.

How long does it take to see results from pest control treatment?

Most customers notice a significant reduction in pest activity within 24-48 hours after treatment. However, complete elimination may take longer depending on the type of pest and severity of infestation. Some pests, like termites or bed bugs, may require multiple treatments for complete elimination.

What if pests return after treatment?

All our service plans include a satisfaction guarantee. If pests return during your warranty period, we'll return and retreat at no additional cost. This is why many customers choose our Protection or Premium Plans for continuous coverage throughout the year.
